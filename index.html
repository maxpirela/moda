Fashion

What you wear is how you present yourself to the world, especially today, when human contacts are so quick. Fashion is instant language.





Sports Wear

Sportswear is nowadays used as a synonym for casual wear. Earlier, only sportsmen used to be seen in the sports clothes but nowadays, with celebrities taking up the trend, everyone is being seen out in their best workout clothes, even without doing a 5-minute workout that day.




Casual Chic

This is an elegant style with clothes chosen mostly in black, white, and neutral colors. The whole look is very classy . In this style one thing you would miss is too much color -you could say, absolutely no garish colors but only elegance.




Lagenlook

In this style of clothing, many layers of unconventionally shaped clothing are worn one on top of the other. There is a certain individuality in the look. This style originated in Europe, to be specific in Germany.
